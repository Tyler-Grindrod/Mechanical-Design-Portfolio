## Featured Projects

### [The Cardiovascular Center of Florida (Healthcare)](./CVCFL-Medical_Office)
* **Scope:** Medicial office building featuring MRI and X-ray suites
* **Challenge:** Managing high heat dissipation from step‑up transformers and sensitive medical equipment
* **Solutions:**  Designed dedicated HVAC zones and specified 4" PVC refrigerant sleeves in chases to support future split‑DX expansion and tenant buildouts
* **Result:** Reduced operating costs through zone isolation and accurate thermal modeling

### [Eagle Lake Apartments (Multi-Family)](./Eagle_Lake_Apartments-(Multi-Family))
* **Scope:** Multi‑building residential complex with amenity clubhouse
* **Challenge:** Coordinating mechanical systems within tight RCP constraints and complex structural layouts
* **Solution:** Used Revit for 3D coordination of duct routing and equipment placement to preserve the architectural vision

### [Drake Water Treatment Plant (Industrial/Utility)](./Drake_Water_Treatment_Plant-(Industrial))
* **Scope:** Specialized HVAC and ventilation for water treatment infrastructure
* **Challenge:** High concentrations of corrosive gases and significant heat dissipation from interior water pumps
* **Solution:** Specified dual wall‑mounted air‑conditioning units with offset temperature controls to reduce equipment wear. Coordinated with civil engineers and vendors to relocate interior chemical storage into corrosion‑resistant, prefabricated fiberglass building

### [Pearl Apartments & Garage (Multi-Family)](./Pearl_apartments_&_Garage-(Multi-Family))
* **Scope:** High‑rise residential tower with enclosed parking garage
* **Challenge:** Tight RCP constraints and limited mechanical space required extensive use of VRF systems. The enclosed garage required sensor‑controlled exhaust triggered by CO and NO₂ detectors
* **Solution:** System ramps from a baseline of 0.05 CFM/sq.ft to 0.75 CFM/sq.ft when sensors detect 25 PPM, ensuring life safety while optimizing energy consumption
